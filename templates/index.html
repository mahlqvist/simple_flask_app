If you want to be good at something, the first and most impotant thing is to set goals and make rules. It's totally up to you how you see yourself or how you picture your future

Get Organized

A great starting point for moving forward is by stopping the behaviors that's holding you back, not by doing more. If you want to improve your personal finance, reduce your spending, before you focus on making more, or you will never stop spending what you have.

To beat the future version of yourself, try to be honest with where you stand. Make a plan, start tracking your progress and you will get there.
